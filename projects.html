Our first project for this school-year was putting together our own PCs. During this process, we were taught what each part of a PC does, and why it is needed for the machine to work. The accompanying image is the final product: a fully working PC!

Project 3: SOHO Network

For our third project, we took a dive into networking, learning about all of the machines that are needed to support a wifi connection.
